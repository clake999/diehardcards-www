Introduction

Welcome to Cardboard Dispatches

Published July 21, 2026

Collecting has always been about more than rows in a database. Every card carries context: the player, the set, the moment, the condition, the hunt, and sometimes a story only its collector knows.

Cardboard Dispatches is a place for those stories and the useful details around them. We’ll share interesting card discoveries, collecting observations, notable developments in the hobby, and occasional market oddities when there is something real and worthwhile to examine.

It will also be our workbench journal for DieHardCards: what we’re learning while building the private-alpha platform, progress on the Hans imaging-station direction, and the thinking behind tools intended to help collectors know their cards and understand their collections.

This first dispatch is simply an introduction. Future entries will arrive when there is something honest, specific, and interesting to share.
