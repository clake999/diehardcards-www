Security

DieHardCards is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users, their collections, and our platform.

Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability, we encourage responsible disclosure.

Please email:

security@diehard.cards

What to Include

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Affected URLs or features
  • Proof-of-concept if available
  • Your contact information

Our Commitment

  • Prompt acknowledgement of reports
  • Investigation and remediation when appropriate
  • Coordination with reporters before public disclosure

Please do not access, modify, or delete data that does not belong to you, perform denial-of-service attacks, or disrupt service availability while testing.
